# Topology of Grounding — Research Program

This repository was previously used to host preprints of papers from
an interdisciplinary research program at the intersection of
mathematics, cognitive science, and philosophy of mind.

The manuscripts have been moved to private working repositories
during the review and publication process, and are not currently
available at this location.

## If you arrived here from a citation or link

If you arrived here from a tweet, citation, blog post, or other
external reference expecting to find specific manuscripts: the
documents are not publicly available here while they are under
review elsewhere.

Public versions of individual papers will be linked from this
page as they reach the appropriate publication stage with
persistent identifiers (DOI, arXiv).
